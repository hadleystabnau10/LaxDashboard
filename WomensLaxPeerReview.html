This dashboard desplays statistics for women’s division 1 lacrosse data from the 2022-2023 season. 120 teams are included in the division among 18 different regional conferences. The data has been plotted to show goals for vs against for each team. Then, clusters were created based on offensive stats for each team, and then those clusters were compared to a plot of defensive stats for each team.

We will be looking at various comparisons among the different teams in both offensive and defensive aspects of the game.

This tab shows some compared offensive data among the different regions. Average goals among conference was not that interesting, so I plotted the winning % for each conference to see a bit more obvious differences. Then a plot was created to show shot volume vs actual shots that landed in the goal.
